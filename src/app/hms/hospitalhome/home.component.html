Preparing to challenge the changes in e-Commerce World

The upcoming changes in the Australian e-Commerce market will have a profound impact on global business conduct.

As of July 1st 2018, the obligation of GST collection is now the responsibility of the foreign seller.

Foreign sellers need to comply with Australian Tax regulations including GST registration and quarterly BAS reporting. Additionally, Reverse logistics will also gain importance with regards to GST drawbacks.

With the use of an all-encompassing Freight Management System and BAS certified employees, JPD Global can help your company comply with these regulations and a lot more.
